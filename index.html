The Future is Electric

Let's Get Ready

Join the Queen's University Relectric Team on our mission to convert combustion engine vehicles to electric powered.

Carbon Emission

The average car in Canada emits 4600 kg of CO2 into the atmosphere per year.

Electric Vehicles

Transport Canada set targets to have 10% of all light-duty cars be electric by 2025, 30% by 2030 and 100% by 2040.

Conversion

We must have a system to refit existing gas powered cars with EV technology to prevent unnnecessary waste.

Vision and Mission

We dream of a sustainable, electric powered future where all internal combustion engine vehicles are repurposed and refitted with high performance technology.

Our Conversion

Relectric will complete the research and development of EV conversion technology and procedure through the conversion of our very own car by undergraduate students.

EV technology is not taught in depth in most universities to undergraduate engineering students. Relectric members will get the opportunity to build this knowledge and ignite a passion in this field that we hope will continue after graduation.

Relectric Global

Team Relectric believes in collaboration. The Queen's branch works closely with the teams at University of Calgary and University of Central Florida to maximize research and innovation.
